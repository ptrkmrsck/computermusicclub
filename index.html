03/26/2025 7-9PM || ARTIST TALK w/ ERIC HEEP

Eric Heep is a performer and composer in Santa Fe, NM. His work generally includes incorporating existing technology in novel ways, coding, and experimental and procedural sound. In his work, he strives to expose the processes and form of natural phenomena. His recent work focuses on spatialized sound, the pairing of volumetric lighting and Wave Field Synthesis, and FM synthesis.

He received his BFA (2014) and MFA (2016) in Music Technology at California Institute of the Arts. He has been special faculty at CalArts since 2020 teaching Music Technology and Algorithmic Composition.

He currently works at Meow Wolf as a Creative Technologist / Technical Artist where he has contributed to over 100 projects and installations.
